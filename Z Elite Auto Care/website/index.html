Engine Component Overhaul

Hands-on disassembly and inspection of engine components. Thorough cleaning, seal replacement, and part reconditioning to restore optimal function and reliability.

CV Axle & Drivetrain Repair

CV axle replacement and drivetrain service including joint inspection and suspension component assessment. Restoring power transfer to wheels for smooth, reliable driving performance.

Engine Valve Adjustment & Timing

Precision valve adjustment and timing work with camshaft inspection. Deep engine maintenance ensuring optimal performance, smooth operation, and extended engine life.

Camshaft Inspection & Timing Service

Detailed camshaft and valve timing inspection with bearing cap assessment. Ensuring precise valve synchronization for optimal engine performance and preventing timing-related issues.

Engine Bay System Repair

Detailed engine bay service including cooling system components, electrical connections, and hose replacements. Hands-on troubleshooting and repair for reliable vehicle operation.

DOHC Camshaft & Valve Train Service

Dual overhead camshaft inspection with valve clearance adjustment and timing verification. Ensuring precise valve operation, proper lubrication, and optimal engine performance.

Complete Engine Removal & Replacement

Full engine extraction using hydraulic hoist for rebuild, replacement, or major overhaul. Expert handling of heavy components with precision and safety for comprehensive repairs.

Dashboard Wiring & Electronics Repair

Comprehensive dashboard disassembly for electrical system repair and diagnostics. Expert troubleshooting of wiring faults, sensor issues, and electronic component installation.

Front Suspension & Brake Assembly Service

Complete front suspension and braking system repair including rotor replacement, wheel bearing service, and control arm inspection. Ensuring safe stopping power and stable handling.

Dashboard Electronics & Module Repair

Dashboard disassembly for electrical system repair and module replacement. Expert handling of wiring, HVAC controls, and safety system components for reliable vehicle electronics.
